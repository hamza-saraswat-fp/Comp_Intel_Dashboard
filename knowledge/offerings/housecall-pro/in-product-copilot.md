# Assessment

Housecall Pro ships two free, default-on conversational copilots that satisfy the "query data + coaching" definition: Analyst AI answers plain-English questions about business data (revenue, job trends, custom reports, filtered customer lists, invoice summaries) and Coach AI gives growth/pricing/hiring guidance. Both are GA, accessible via chat, and included on every plan at no extra cost. This is the broadest GA in-product copilot of the three competitors for this capability. Depth is strong (not market_leading) because reasoning depth is lighter than ServiceTitan's data-grounding ambitions, but the copilots are genuinely GA and core to the product.
