# Assessment

ServiceTitan's agentic-office capability is now real and in-market as a private preview, not just a keynote roadmap. Four Atlas office modules (Marketing Pro, Contact Center Pro, Adaptive Capacity, Accounting) entered private preview in the Fall 2025 release, available to select accounts via waitlist, and there is published production usage: a ServiceTitan customer success story (Quality Service Company, May 28 2026) shows Atlas building Adaptive Capacity booking rules in ~30 minutes (versus two weeks manually), with ~30% of that customer's bookings flowing end-to-end with no human involvement.
