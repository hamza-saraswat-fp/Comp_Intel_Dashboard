# Assessment

Atlas is a GA conversational AI assistant that ServiceTitan's help center describes as a Core product available to all ServiceTitan customers, surfaced today in the mobile app for technicians, in Field Pro, and in the Help Center. Its GA scope is field-technician Q&A over ServiceTitan data in plain English; only the equipment-manual/diagnostic modules require a Field Pro subscription, so the copilot is not wholly gated behind Field Pro tiers. The office-side Atlas modules (Accounting, Marketing Pro, Contact Center Pro, Field Pro Sales Coach) are further along than the public marketing page's stale 'Coming soon' label implies: the help center lists them as Private Preview, open to select accounts by waitlist. Real and data-grounded, but the full office copilot is still limited-access, which holds this at strong rather than market_leading.

# Detail

GA today (per the help center Atlas Home page): Atlas answers technician questions by pulling ServiceTitan data through a conversational interface and 'is already available in the mobile apps for technicians, Field Pro, and the Help Center.' The help center states 'Atlas is a Core product available to all ServiceTitan customers' — so base Atlas is NOT tied to Field Pro. What IS Field-Pro-gated is the equipment/diagnostics layer: the help center notes 'the Troubleshooting and Equipment Information modules are only available in Field Pro,' and Field Pro Standard is what 'gives technicians Atlas-powered diagnostics and equipment lookups' (nameplate scans, manuals, wiring diagrams, guided diagnostics). So packaging is only partly tied to Field Pro tiers — the correction to the prior 'tied to Field Pro tiers' framing.

Office modules — Private Preview, not merely 'Coming soon': the public Atlas features page (servicetitan.com/features/atlas) still carries the 'Atlas in the Office (Coming soon)' marketing heading, but the help center describes these as live in limited preview: 'Private Preview features — including Atlas in Accounting, Marketing Pro, Contact Center Pro, and Field Pro Sales Coach — are available to select accounts only. To request access for your account, join the waitlist.' These office modules do office-side work (Accounting: automatic invoice review for missing info/anomalies with recommended next steps; Marketing Pro: flags missed-revenue opportunities and drafts/launches campaigns; Contact Center Pro: conversational access to call-center reporting and high-priority-call surfacing; Field Pro Sales Coach). Announced at Pantheon 2025 (Sept 18, 2025) and reiterated in the Fall 2025 release (Oct 13, 2025), which frames the rollout as ongoing ('Atlas will continue to roll out across ServiceTitan').

Net: field/help-center copilot is GA and broadly available; office copilot is private-preview (limited-access), not GA and no longer just roadmap. More data-grounded than Jobber/HCP copilots, but because the office copilot is still preview-gated, this stays strong, not market_leading.
