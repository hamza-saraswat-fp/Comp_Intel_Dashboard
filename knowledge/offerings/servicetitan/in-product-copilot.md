# Assessment

Atlas is a GA conversational AI assistant that lets users query ServiceTitan data in plain English, but its shipped scope is field-technician Q&A (customer/equipment history, manuals, diagnostics via Field Pro). The broader analytics/coaching copilot 'Atlas in the Office' (manager opportunity-spotting, dispatcher/CSR assist, AR invoice review) is explicitly labeled 'Coming soon,' so the in-product copilot is real and data-grounded but field-scoped today.
