# State of the field

All three vendors genuinely ship the rarer capability here — AI-DRAFTED review responses, not just automated solicitation — which makes this a relatively even, mature field. ServiceTitan leads on breadth and depth: its Titan Intelligence Review Response Generator offers named tone (Professional/Casual/Enthusiastic) and length controls plus a custom-instruction field, and it pairs AI drafting with consolidated monitoring across 60+ listing sites and review-to-technician/customer/job attribution analytics. Housecall Pro and Jobber both confirm AI-generated responses too, but their monitoring is narrower (HCP: Google/Facebook; Jobber: Google Business Profile), and HCP's generator is a general-purpose "Write it for Me" tool rather than a review-specialized one. Net: ServiceTitan is the strongest of the three on reputation, with HCP and Jobber close behind — none is so far ahead as to be market-leading, since the headline AI-draft feature is now table stakes across all three.
