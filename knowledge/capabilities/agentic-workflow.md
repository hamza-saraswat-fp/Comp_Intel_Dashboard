# State of the field

No FSM vendor has shipped true multi-step, goal-seeking agents that run entire office workflows end-to-end — the category is still emergent. ServiceTitan has by far the most ambitious agentic vision (Atlas in the Office: autonomously reviewing AR invoices, setting dispatcher capacity rules, throttling/launching marketing, auto-creating RFIs and change orders), but its own Atlas page explicitly labels these "Coming soon," so they are roadmap, not usable today. Housecall Pro and Jobber both ship real but narrow autonomy: HCP's CSR AI autonomously answers, qualifies, and books jobs 24/7 in a single domain (a paid add-on), and Jobber runs trigger/condition/action automations plus AI-drafted quotes with human oversight. The leader on vision is ServiceTitan; the leaders on actually-shipped (if bounded) autonomy are HCP and Jobber — but all three sit at "basic" because none yet runs autonomous cross-functional office workflows.
