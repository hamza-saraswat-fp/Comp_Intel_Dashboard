# State of the field

All three vendors now ship genuine AI customer-contact agents that answer inbound calls AND text/chat 24/7, qualify leads, and book directly into the calendar — this is one of the most mature AI areas in FSM and a real strength for each. ServiceTitan leads: beyond its GA AI Voice Agent (books via real-time Adaptive Capacity, with an SMS Agent in Marketing Pro), it offers Contact Center Pro — an enterprise omni-channel stack with AI Manager Assist (call summaries, sentiment) — giving it a depth and breadth neither peer matches. Jobber and Housecall Pro are close behind with strong, GA, well-priced voice receptionists (Jobber Receptionist launched Aug 2025 at $99/mo; HCP's CSR AI markets a 42x ROI), but both are single-product receptionists rather than full AI contact centers. The net: ServiceTitan is market-leading here; Jobber and HCP are strong and competitive at the SMB tier; FieldPulse should expect this to be table stakes, not a differentiator, among top FSM rivals.
