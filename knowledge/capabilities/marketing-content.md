# State of the field

On AI marketing-content generation, the two SMB-focused players lead today: Housecall Pro and Jobber both ship genuine LLM content creation that drafts marketing emails, SMS, social posts, and service descriptions from a short prompt plus the customer's own business data, and both are generally available now. Housecall Pro's edge is packaging (Marketing AI / "Write it for me" is free and default-on across all plans); Jobber's edge is depth of orchestration (an AI-generated marketing plan, full campaign drafts, and a Rewrite tone-restyler), gated behind its paid ~$79/mo Marketing Suite. ServiceTitan is the most sophisticated on data-driven automation and ad optimization (Ads Optimizer feeds revenue data into Google Ads to target high-value customers), but its actual generative content layer — the "Email Content Generator" and "Audience Assistant" — is still listed as "Coming soon" on the Titan Intelligence page, so on generative marketing content specifically ServiceTitan currently trails the two SMB vendors. No vendor is a clear category leader on generative marketing content yet, so none rates market-leading.
