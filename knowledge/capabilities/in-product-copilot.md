# State of the field

All three vendors ship a conversational, plain-English in-app assistant, but they sit at different maturity points. Housecall Pro leads on this specific capability: Analyst AI (data/report queries) and Coach AI (growth/pricing/hiring coaching) are both fully GA, free, and on-by-default for every plan, directly matching the "query your data + get coaching in plain English" definition. ServiceTitan's Atlas is the most data-grounded assistant but its GA scope is field-technician Q&A (equipment/diagnostics); the office analytics/coaching copilot ("Atlas in the Office," TI Chat) is explicitly "Coming soon" and not yet usable, so its in-product-copilot footprint is narrower today than the marketing implies. Jobber's Copilot is a credible coach/data-analyst/product-expert assistant on web and mobile, but as of mid-2026 it is still officially labeled Beta in Jobber's own help center. Net: HCP is broadest-shipped, ServiceTitan is deepest-but-field-scoped, Jobber is solid-but-still-beta.
