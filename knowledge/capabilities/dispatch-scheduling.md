# State of the field

ServiceTitan is the clear and lone leader in true ML-driven dispatch: Dispatch Pro (Titan Intelligence) is GA and uses machine learning to predict job value and technician performance, then recommends/assigns technicians to maximize revenue — a genuine predictive engine, not GPS heuristics, and ServiceTitan's single strongest AI differentiator in this category. Jobber and Housecall Pro both lag with no native ML dispatch: their "smart scheduling" is manual drag-and-drop plus map/GPS visualization. Jobber adds heuristic route optimization (sequencing by drive time, traffic, road closures, and team GPS location) but no skill/workload-based ML technician assignment; Housecall Pro has no native route optimization at all, offloading it to the paid third-party Beeline Routes integration. Net: ServiceTitan is market-leading, while both SMB-focused vendors score "none" on AI dispatch as defined (true ML), making this the widest AI gap in ServiceTitan's favor.
