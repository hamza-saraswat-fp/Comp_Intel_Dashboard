# Jobber — Pricing & Packaging Profile
*Prepared for FieldPulse · July 2026*

Jobber positions itself as an all-in-one operations platform for home and commercial service businesses — from solo contractors up through 15+ person teams and multi-location operators — spanning trades like lawn care, cleaning, HVAC, plumbing, and landscaping. It uses a classic land-and-expand SaaS ladder: a cheap single-user entry plan (Core) to win solo operators, then steep price jumps at Connect and Grow gated behind features teams actually need (QuickBooks sync, two-way SMS, job costing, automation), topped by a premium Plus tier that bundles AI and marketing tools free. Jobber prices at the higher end of the FSM category — its published range runs from $29/mo (Core, annual, 1 user) up to $529–699/mo (Plus, annual/monthly, 15 users) — and layers a growing list of paid AI and marketing add-ons on top of the base subscription (as of Jul 2026, verified directly on getjobber.com/pricing).

## Plans

Jobber sells both **Individual** (1 user) and **Team** (multi-user) variants of most plans, each with three billing options: pay-monthly (no commitment), 1-year commitment paid monthly, and pay-annually (billed upfront, cheapest per month). Figures below are the Team configuration most comparable to FieldPulse's target segment; Individual pricing is noted separately.

| Plan | Monthly (no commitment) | Annual (billed upfront) | Users | Headline gates |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Core** (Individual only) | $49/mo | $29/mo | 1 | Online booking/scheduling, quoting, invoicing, online payments |
| **Connect** (Team, 5 users) | $199/mo | $149/mo | 5 | Adds automated reminders, automatic payment collection, checklists, QuickBooks sync, time tracking |
| **Grow** (Team, 10 users) | $399/mo | $299/mo | 10 | Adds customizable visual quotes, upsell line items, automatic time tracking, two-way SMS, workflow automation |
| **Plus** (Team, 10 users) | $599/mo | $449/mo | 10 | Adds centralized files/media, team mentions, automated job costing, Pipeline (sales CRM board), Marketing Suite, AI Receptionist — all included free; dedicated onboarding, premium support |

*Individual (1-user) equivalents, annual billing: Connect $99/mo, Grow $149/mo. Team plans also scale to 5/10/15-user brackets with prices rising accordingly (e.g., Plus 15-user is $529/mo annual, $699/mo month-to-month). Extra users beyond a plan's included count: **$29/mo per user**, any plan. (getjobber.com/pricing, fetched Jul 2026)*

## Payment processing

Published directly on getjobber.com (Jul 2026):
- **Card-present (tap to pay):** 2.7% + $0.30 per transaction
- **Card-not-present (online/invoiced payments):** 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **ACH / bank transfer:** 1% per transaction (US only)
- **Instant payouts:** additional 1% fee (vs. standard ~2-business-day payout)

## Recent pricing changes

- **Aug 18, 2025** — Jobber AI Receptionist reached general availability as a paid add-on, following a beta that had reportedly processed 200,000+ conversations. Initial reporting and multiple pricing round-up/review sites (still circulating as of Jul 2026) cite a **$99/mo flat** add-on price on Grow and lower tiers. Source: PR Newswire launch release, Aug 2025.
- **Sep 25, 2025** — At Jobber's "Jobber Now" annual product event, the company announced **Jobber Voice** (hands-free voice assistant), **Campaign Generator** (AI email campaign builder, part of Marketing Suite), and **AI-powered quote automation** (auto-drafted quotes, high-value quote alerts) for Connect/Grow/Plus web-app users. No new AI Receptionist pricing was announced at this event. Source: PR Newswire, Sep 2025.
- **Undated, sometime between the Aug 2025 launch and Jul 2026** — AI Receptionist pricing changed from the reported flat **$99/mo** to a usage-based **$29/mo base including 30 conversations, with $0.79 per additional conversation** thereafter. **This is the current state confirmed via a direct live fetch of getjobber.com/pricing in Jul 2026**, which shows "$29/mo" for Receptionist with no visible fine print on usage terms on the pricing page itself. The specific $0.79/conversation overage rate and 30-conversation included quota are corroborated by multiple independent secondary sources (a Jobber help-center article title match plus several review/pricing-breakdown sites) but could **not** be directly verified against Jobber's own help center, which returned HTTP 403 to automated fetches; **the exact date of the repricing could not be established** — treat the $0.79/conversation and 30-conversation figures as "(reported, unverified by vendor)" even though the $29/mo base price itself is vendor-confirmed.
- **Ongoing** — Multiple third-party pricing trackers describe a pattern of incremental annual price increases and periodic Connect-tier restructuring since 2022; no single dated announcement was found for a comprehensive tier repricing in 2026, but Core's no-commitment monthly price ($49) is notably higher than its annual price ($29), consistent with margin being pushed toward commitment terms over time.

## Packaging observations

- **AI is being unbundled from a premium tier into an à la carte, usage-priced add-on.** The AI Receptionist's apparent move from a $99/mo flat fee to a $29/mo-plus-usage model lowers the entry price (better top-of-funnel conversion for AI upsell) while preserving — and likely increasing — expansion revenue from heavy users via the $0.79/conversation overage. This is a "land cheap, expand on usage" pattern rather than flat-fee AI pricing.
- **Plus is the AI/marketing bundle tier.** AI Receptionist, Pipeline, and Marketing Suite are all included free at Plus ($449–599/mo annual depending on user count) but cost $29+$49+$79 = $157/mo combined as standalone add-ons elsewhere — a strong incentive to upgrade rather than assemble add-ons piecemeal.
- **Per-user pricing compounds with tier-gating.** Extra users are a flat $29/mo regardless of plan, but the bigger lever is that adding even one employee to a solo Core-plan account forces an upgrade to a materially pricier Connect/Grow team plan — the Individual→Team jump is steep (e.g., Grow Individual $149/mo annual vs. Grow Team-5 $229/mo annual for the same feature set).
- **Voice/hands-free AI is being bundled into base plans, not sold as an add-on** — Jobber Voice rolled out "across all mobile app subscription tiers" with no disclosed separate price, suggesting Jobber differentiates its AI monetization strategy by feature: conversational/lead-capture AI (Receptionist) is metered and monetized directly, while in-app productivity AI (Voice, auto-drafted quotes) is bundled to drive retention and tier upgrades instead.
- **Add-ons are deliberately decoupled from annual billing** — they're monthly-only even on annual base plans, which both simplifies Jobber's billing logic and keeps add-on revenue growing at list price without the ~30-40% annual discount applied to the base subscription.
